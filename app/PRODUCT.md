# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Operators and administrators of Italian third-sector entities (associations, cooperatives, foundations, ETS) — typically 35-60, not highly technical, working from a desktop browser during office hours. Consultants who manage multiple entities. Their job: find relevant grants, evaluate fit, track applications.

## Product Purpose
Match public and private grants to entity profiles using a 6-dimension scoring engine (themes, legal form, territory, capacity, documents, track record). Surface the best opportunities first, provide AI-powered analysis, and track application pipelines. Success = the user finds and applies to more grants with less manual research.

## Brand Personality
Authoritative, trustworthy, efficient. The interface communicates institutional competence — this is a professional tool for serious funding decisions, not a consumer app. Calm confidence, not excitement.

## Anti-references
- Playful/gamified UIs (Duolingo, gamification patterns)
- Overly colorful SaaS dashboards (Monday.com neon palette)
- Generic admin templates (AdminLTE, bland Bootstrap dashboards)
- Startup landing pages with gradients and hero animations
- Overly dense enterprise UIs (SAP, Oracle)

## Design Principles
1. **Clarity over decoration** — Every element earns its space. Information density where needed, whitespace where it helps scanning.
2. **Trust through consistency** — Same button shapes, same badge vocabulary, same spacing rhythm. The tool disappears into the task.
3. **Score-first hierarchy** — The matching score is the primary information. All visual hierarchy serves it.
4. **Progressive disclosure** — Show summary first, expand to detail. Don't overwhelm on first glance.
5. **Italian institutional tone** — UI copy is formal but not bureaucratic. Professional Italian, not marketing Italian.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. Body text ≥ 4.5:1 contrast. Touch targets ≥ 44px on mobile. All interactive elements keyboard-accessible. Reduced-motion alternatives for any animation.
